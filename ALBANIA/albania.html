Overview of Albania

Albania is a country located in southeastern Europe on the Balkan peninsula. It shares borders with
Montenegro, Kosovo(Albania), North Macedonia and Greece. Albania has beautiful coastlines along both the Adriatic and Ionian seas.
The capital and largest city in Albania is Tirana, it is known for its high tourist attraction, vibrant energy, colourful architecture and shocking history.
Albania's landscape is super diverse, featuring mountains,hills and pristine beaches. The ALbanian alps in the north are known for their dramitic and stunning scenery
and are a popular destination for hikers and tourists. Along the south coast, the Albanian Riviera is famous for its crystal-clear waters and seaside villages. The rich variety
of Albania’s natural environment contributes to its reputation as a destination of exceptional beauty.Modern Albania is known for its warm hospitality and growing tourism industry.
In recent years, it has gained international attention as an emerging travel destination, attracting an estimated 10–12 million visitors each year. Tourists are drawn to its unspoiled nature,
historical landmarks, and affordable prices compared to other Mediterranean destinations.
