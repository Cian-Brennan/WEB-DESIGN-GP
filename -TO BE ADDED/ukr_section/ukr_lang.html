Ukrainian Culture

Language

Ukrainian (українська мова) is the official language of Ukraine and one of the most melodic Slavic languages. It belongs to the East Slavic group, together with Belarusian and Russian, but it has many unique features that distinguish it. Ukrainian uses the Cyrillic alphabet, with several letters and sounds that are not present in other Slavic languages, giving it a characteristic rhythm and softness. The language is known for vowel harmony, expressive diminutives, and a rich tradition of folk poetry, songs, and storytelling.
